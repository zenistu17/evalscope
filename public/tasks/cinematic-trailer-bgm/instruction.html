Your objective is to produce a Suspenseful Orchestral Theme of Emotion with Dark Orchestration with Epic Fantasy attributes similar to those used in the Game of Thrones series and other Epic Fantasy films.

This project aims to develop a "full trailer audio" through the combination of all of the various stems.
